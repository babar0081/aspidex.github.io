Welcome to our Texpeed Marlon Brando style leather jacket, exclusive to us! This popular design was originally showcased in the movie classic of the 50's "The Wild One"

The star actor Marlon Brando can be seen rocking this stylish design in various scenes, fast forward many years and its still a popular design in today's era.

We've kept all the important design however some modern features have been added to suit the roads of today.

  • Top-grain leather - great quality with a nice feel to it
  • YKK metal zips - made to stand the test of time
  • Stay Protected - CE approved armour to the back, shoulders and elbows, which is totally removable
  • Keep your contents safe with our x3 zipped pockets & x1 press stud pocket
  • 6 months Texpeed brand assurance warranty
  • Water resistant
  • Double layer waistband for extra durability
  • Trouser attachment zip - reduces the cold chills up your spine
